How To Use Biblio

Quick guide for users!

How to add books?

Go to the book adding page by clicking the +Add button in the menu,
then enter the 13 digits of isbn code. (with or without hyphen)

Check out the content, update if you want and register your book by clicking add book button!

How to borrow books?

Go to home by clicking Biblio Logo , Search for your favorite books.
If you find a favorite one, contact with book's owner by clicking the "Request" button.
A request mail will be automatically sent out to the owner so that you can communicate with him/her where and when to borrow the book.

Once you have the book, click Borrow button so that the status of this book become 'lent out' and you can see all of the borrowed books in a borrowed page

How to return books?

Got to the borrowed books page.
Once you returned the book, click the 'Return' button so that the status of this book become available.
